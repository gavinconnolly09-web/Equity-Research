# Equity-Research
This repository contains independent equity research projects focused on public companies across differnet sectors. The purpose of this project is to develop and demonstrate practical investment research, valuation, and financial analysis skills through concise company deep dives and investment theses.

Each company folder includes:
- Company overview and business model analysis
- Financial performance analysis
- Peer and valuation comparisons
- Risk and catalyst assessment
- Discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation models
- Final investment thesis and recommendation

The goal of the project is not to create overly long institutional-style reports, but rather to build scalable, investment-oriented equity snapshots that combine:
- Fundamental analysis
- Valuation analysis
- Market sentiment and investor behavior
- Strategic and industry positioning
- Long-term investment reasoning

A major focus of the research process is understanding how changing business quality, market narratives, profitability trends, and investor expectations influence valuation and investment outlooks over time.

This repository also serves as a personal learning platform to strengthen skills in:
- Equity research
- Financial modeling
- Valuation
- Market analysis
- Investment thesis development
- Industry and competitive analysis

All analysis is based on publicly available information, including company filings, earnings reports, investor presentations, financial platforms, and industry research.
